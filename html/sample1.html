Tractor:

    Indian farmers can surely buy SIMBA 30 when they need a mini tractor in India that can help them in every agriculture-related task. Manufactured by CNH Industrial, SIMBA 30 has ample impressive specifications that make it deliver a powerful performance.
    Because of its advanced specification, SIMBA 30 becomes a very famous mini tractor model in India in the 29 HP tractor category. 

    Features of CNH Industrial SIMBA 30:-

       - To begin with, the tractor has a 29 HP engine with a displacement of 1318 CC. 
            - The SIMBA 30 mini tractor features a 9 Forward + 3 Reverse gearbox, which empowers the tractor so much that it can perform well in all sorts of operating conditions.
            - What attracted us and many others is the unique and eye-catching design and strong construction. As premium-grade metal is used to manufacture it, this tractor is built to excel and perform for years to come. 
            - The tractor comes with two PTO speeds, 540 & 1000 RPM. 
            - Speaking of its dimensions, the tractor has a wide wheelbase of 1500 mm, weighs 960 KG, and uses tyres of sizes F(5.00X12), and R(8.00X18). 
            - The SIMBA 30 boasts an impressive lifting capacity of up to 800 kg. So, farmers can use this mini tractor for heavy load lifting as well. 
            - The CNH Industrial SIMBA 30 Small tractor price in India is Rs. 5.00 - 5.65 Lakhs*
